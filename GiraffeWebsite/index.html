Ranking Giraffes for my Wife

8. Angolan giraffe

The Angolan giraffe is by far my least favorite. It looks like a mock giraffe. As if a some kind of long next animal decided to roll around in some dirt to get the same spots as a normal giraffe.

3/10

7. Nubian giraffe

The Nubian giraffe is a little better than the one prior. It looks like it actually wants to be a giraffe. It's spots are more defined and look like they're a part of it, not like some kind of makeup.

5/10

6. West African giraffe

Now with the West African giraffe you can easily tell the spots are more rectangular. It has good color and a nice gentle face. It still though, looks a little dull.

5/10

5. South African giraffe

The South African giraffe is great. I love how rectangular the spots are ,and the colors are nice and rich. It's not the best but it's pretty dadgum good.

7/10

4. Kordofan giraffe

Kordofan is amazong looking. It has dark, rich, well-defined spots. Along with all that, it's name is pretty fun to say. It kinda sounds like something out of Star Wars.

8/10

3. Luangwa giraffe

This one is basically on the same scale as the Kordofan. It's name is cool and it has spots that kinda flare out like a flower.

8/10

2. Masai giraffe

The Masai giraffe is fucking fantastic. It looks cool as shit. Cool color, cool name, cool ass spots.

9/10

1. Reticulated giraffe

This is THE giraffe. No others even come close to comparing to this beauty. If there was racism amoung giraffes, this would be the founder of the KKK.

10/10
